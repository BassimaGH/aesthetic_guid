Thoughts

Aesthetics is the philosophical study of beauty and taste (figure 1.1). The way I define it is that it is mostly associated with how people view art works and designs. Their judgement on whether it is beautiful or ugly is based on their social status, background, cultures, and personalities.

Tools

Design tools are used to help us design in a cohesive manner. There are various tools that help us atchive the desired results. For example, the gestalt principles help us compose designs principles commonly associated with the gestalt theory (figure 3.1). Other examples of more tools are: contrast, balance, emphasis, proportion, hierarchy, repetition, rhythm, pattern, white space, movement, variety, and unity.
