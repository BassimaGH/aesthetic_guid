Thoughts

Aesthetics is the philosophical study of beauty and taste (figure 1.1). The way I define it is that it is mostly associated with how people view art works and designs. Their judgement on whether it is beautiful or ugly is based on their social status, background, cultures, and personalities.

Insperation

Insperation results in the production of innovative or creative ideas. It is usually a diffirent process for each person as it depends on their personal experiences, cultural background, and more. For me I tend to seek Insperation for creative works from Pintrest and other platforms that provides a genre, color, or product in forms of pictures (figure 2.1). This helps me open my mind to other new possibilites associated with my current work.

Tools

Design tools are used to help us design in a cohesive manner. There are various tools that help us atchive the desired results. For example, the gestalt principles help us compose designs principles commonly associated with the gestalt theory (figure 3.1). Other examples of more tools are: contrast, balance, emphasis, proportion, hierarchy, repetition, rhythm, pattern, white space, movement, variety, and unity.

figure 3.1

Questions

Here is a list of questions to ask the client to know about their aesthetic preference:

  • 1- What are your goals?
  • 2- What are your values?
  • 3- How do you precive the asthetic related to your (insert client's need)?
  • 4- What are your skills?
  • 5- What pleases you?
  • 6- What are your intresets?
  • 7- What is drives your motivation?
  • 8- What do you thing is missing from your (insert client's need)?
  • 9- What are the conditions for using the (insert client's need)?
  • 10- Who are the target group for your (insert client's need)?
